Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Principles

Friday is built with a local-first philosophy. We believe your desktop data should stay on your machine. Our goal is to provide a powerful assistant without compromising your privacy.

2. Data Collection

Local Data: Friday processes your files, system information, and session history locally. This data is stored on your computer and is not uploaded to our servers.

Authentication: We use Clerk for user authentication. When you sign in, Clerk manages your identity data according to their privacy policy. Friday only receives the necessary tokens to verify your session.

3. AI Processing

Friday interacts with AI models (like Gemini) via API. Only the specific prompts and context required to fulfill your requests are sent to the AI provider. We do not use your data to train our own models.

4. Security

Friday uses system-level encryption to protect your stored session data and any secrets (like API keys) you provide within the application.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us through our community channels or the GitHub repository.
