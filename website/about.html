About Friday

Built by me. For everyone.

Click to meet me 🤝

Friday started as a personal project to answer a simple question: what if your computer could just… do things for you?

The problem

Most AI tools sit in a browser tab waiting for you to come to them. You copy text in, paste text out, switch windows, repeat. It's better than nothing, but it's not what AI should feel like.

The real promise of AI is a computer that understands what you're trying to do and helps you do it, right where you are, without you having to change how you work.

The idea

Friday is built around a different model. It lives on your desktop, always a hotkey away. You talk to it, literally, with your voice, and it acts. It can see your screen, control your apps, browse the web, and run tasks in the background.

No new interface to learn. No tab-switching. Just say what you need and get back to work.

What we believe

01

AI should come to you

You shouldn't have to change your workflow to use AI. Friday meets you where you are, on your desktop, in your apps, right now.

02

Voice is the most natural interface

Typing is overhead. Talking is thinking out loud. Friday is built voice-first because that's how people naturally delegate work.

03

Privacy by default

Friday runs locally on your machine. What happens on your computer stays on your computer, no cloud required for core tasks.

04

AI for everyone

Powerful AI tools shouldn't require a technical background to use. Friday is designed to feel as natural as talking to a colleague.
