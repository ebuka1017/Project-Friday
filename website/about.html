The Vision of Friday

Friday is more than just a chatbot. It's a new way to interact with your digital world.

Bridging the Gap

For too long, AI has been confined to chat windows and web interfaces. We believe that for AI to be truly useful, it needs to have a physical presence on your workspace. It needs to understand the files you're working on, the windows you have open, and the tasks you repeat every single day.

Native & Secure

Project Friday is built from the ground up to be a native Windows application. By living directly in your OS, it can perform actions that web-based assistants simply can't. From organizing your messy downloads folder to refactoring code across multiple project files, Friday acts as a force multiplier for your productivity.

Privacy First

In an age of data harvesting, our commitment to privacy is unwavering. Friday is designed to process your local context securely. Your files and system data stay on your machine, using industry-standard encryption for all session data.
